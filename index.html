Estimated at ~30% of gross. Other fundraiser types provide $0 in included program materials.

  1. Choose a fundraiser type to set a realistic default baseline.
  2. Enter your student count, participation rate, and average donation.

  5. Results and the comparison table update after clicking Calculate.

Pro tip: Apex-managed events typically raise significantly more than DIY because of built-in incentives, logistics, and on-campus energy — so starting with the right baseline matters.
